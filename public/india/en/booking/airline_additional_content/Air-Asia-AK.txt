<h3>FAQs</h3><b>1.Does Air Asia charge any cancellation fee?</b><p>
Yes, Air Asia does charge a booking cancellation fee. The fee depends on a number of things such as the place of departure, the destination you are traveling to, class, sector, etc.</p><b>2.
Till when can I make changes in my reservations?</b><p>
Customers can make changes up to 48 hours prior to the scheduled time of the departure of the flight.</p><b>3.
Is name change allowed while making changes in your bookings?</b><p>
No, Air Asia does not permit name change.</p>
